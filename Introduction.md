
CoreInventory

Overview

CoreInventory is a simple Inventory Management System (IMS) designed to manage and track stock operations within a business. It replaces manual registers and spreadsheets with a centralized digital system that helps monitor inventory in real time.

Purpose

The goal of this project is to simplify inventory handling by allowing users to track product stock, incoming goods, outgoing deliveries, and internal stock movements in one place.

Main Features

- Product management with SKU, category, and unit of measure
- Incoming stock management (Receipts)
- Outgoing stock management (Delivery Orders)
- Internal stock transfers between locations
- Inventory adjustments for correcting stock mismatches
- Dashboard with key inventory statistics
- Multi-warehouse support and stock tracking by location

Inventory Flow

1. Receive goods from vendors → stock increases
2. Move stock between warehouse locations
3. Deliver products to customers → stock decreases
4. Adjust stock for damaged or missing items

All inventory activities are recorded in a stock ledger to maintain a complete history of stock movement.

Users

- Inventory Managers – monitor and manage overall inventory
- Warehouse Staff – handle stock movement, picking, packing, and counting

Goal

CoreInventory aims to provide a simple, efficient, and organized way to manage inventory operations for businesses.
